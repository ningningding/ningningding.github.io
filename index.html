    I am a Tenure-Track Assistant Professor in the Data Science and Analytics Thrust and the Internet of Things Thrust, Information Hub, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). I also hold an affiliated position at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Clear Water Bay).

    I received my PhD from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong SAR, China, advised by Prof. Jianwei Huang. Before joining HKUST-GZ, I was a Postdoctoral Scholar at Northwestern University, Evanston, USA, advised by Prof. Randall Berry and Prof. Ermin Wei.

    Research Interests:
    My research focuses on interdisciplinary areas of artificial intelligence, network systems, and network economics.
    • Artificial intelligence: federated learning, machine unlearning, multi-agent coordination and game.
    • Network system: Internet of Things (IoT).
    • Network economics: data trading, mechanism design.
